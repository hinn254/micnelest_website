Our Security Services

Micnelest (K) Limited is a non-stop service center highly involved in the following security services tailor made to suit the the client's desires

  • Man guarding

    Our company has well trained staff members who provide man guarding services for homes, Residential Estates, Offices, Private and Public properties, Institutions and Events. We offer 24-Hour surveillance and patrol with radio call services.

  • Alarm installations and response

    We install alarm systems at our client's premises with strategically placed trigger points. We have 24-Hour standby response team to respond to distress calls.

  • Dog unit

    We offer guard dog services. These range from breeding, training and selling of Pedigree and registered dog breeds.

  • CCTV systems

    Procurement, supply and installation of closed-circuit television camera systems is our forte. We have developed a highly automated surveillance system for use in accordance to a client's requirement. We also provide repair and maintenance services for the installed systems.

  • VIP protection

    Our Company has highly trained bodyguards and official drivers to offer security services for VIP or clients who require physical protection. This extends to their travelling parties, families and acquaintances.

  • Motion detectors and movement restrictors

    As part of increasingly innovative services, we offer procurement, supply and installation of motion detectors. Movement restriction is highly technical method of security and restricting movement of valuable items such as art and museum pieces to prevent theft. Motion detectors are meant to trigger alarms in case of encroachment towards a restricted area.

  • Cash in transit

    We provide courier services for cash and valuables in transit in collaboration with other security agencies e.g. the Kenya Police.

  • Health & Safety management

    Our client's safety and well-being remain our core business. Therefore, we have developed Health and Safety management packages to offer emergency response in case of fire, accident and other tragedies. We also train on fire exit drills, First Aid and medical evacuation.

  • Fire Equipment

    Micnelest (K) Limited supplies and installs fire equipment including fire extinguishers, fire alarms, Hose pipes and fire hydrants.
